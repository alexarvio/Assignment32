Nagasaki and Hiroshima

The article introduces ideas on what the Japanese society could look now if they would have not suffered from two atomic bomb blasts during the second World War.

As we know, two cities in Japan were almost completely destroyed by the atomic bombs detonated by the Americans. These cities are Hiroshima and Nagasaki.

Ass seen in the picture, Hiroshima was completely destroyed after the bomb detonated. The bomb was name Little boy by the Americans and killed 146,000 souls.

The Nagasaki bomb was named Fat Man and took the lives of around 80,000 people.

Japan declared that the war was over for them. This attack has severe consequences for the empire and left the Japanese society scarred for life.
